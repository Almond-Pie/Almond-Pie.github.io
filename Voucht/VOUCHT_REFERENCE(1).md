## 1. What Voucht Is

Voucht is a managed local dominance service for independent high-street businesses. We help cafés, restaurants, barbers, nail salons, gyms, takeaways, and small shops get more Google reviews, generate customer content, climb local search rankings, and attract more foot traffic.

### The Core Product (four things)

1. **A branded hub page** (hosted by Voucht) linking the business's Google reviews, socials, menu, website, events, and content raffle. NFC/QR pads on tables drive customers to this page.

2. **A weekly competitive snapshot** showing the business how they rank for relevant "near me" searches — their position, review count, competitor data, and movement week over week.

3. **A UGC content library with customer raffle** (Growth tier). Customers snap photos/videos of their experience via the hub page and are entered into a weekly prize draw. The business gets a growing library of authentic customer-generated content for their social media, website, and marketing.

4. **Branded NFC/QR pads** installed on tables and counters. The physical mechanism driving scans, reviews, and content creation.

### Core Positioning

**"We help local businesses dominate their area on Google."**

The pads are the delivery mechanism. The hub page is the product. The weekly snapshot is the retention engine. The UGC library is the content engine. The subscription is the business model.

### What We're Selling

Two outcomes, not features:

**Outcome 1 — Visibility:** "Get more reviews than your competitors and Google sends you more customers. Every week you see exactly where you rank and how you're climbing."

**Outcome 2 — Content:** "Your customers become your content team. They snap photos of your food, your coffee, your venue — you get a library of real content to post on Instagram, TikTok, and everywhere else. And it brings them back because they're entered into a weekly raffle."

### What We Are NOT

- NOT an NFC card seller
- NOT a review generation tool (we don't fake, gate, or incentivise reviews)
- NOT a marketing agency
- NOT a software platform (yet — managed service transitioning to SaaS)

### Who We Serve

- Independent cafés, restaurants, takeaways
- Barbers, nail salons, beauty businesses
- Gyms, studios, wellness businesses
- Small retail shops with a physical location
- Any high-street business that can receive Google reviews
- **NOT** chains, franchises, or online-only businesses

### Taglines

- Primary: **"Let your customers vouch for you."**
- Secondary: **"Your business, one scan away."**
- Urgency: **"Your competitors are already ahead."**
- Outcome: **"Dominate your area. One scan at a time."**

## 4. The UGC Content Library & Customer Raffle

### Overview

This is a Growth-tier feature that solves two problems at once: it gives the business a constant stream of authentic customer-generated content, and it gives customers a reason to engage with the pad on every visit (not just their first).

### How It Works

The hub page has two separate paths:

**Path 1 — Reviews (all tiers, NOT incentivised):**
"Leave us a review" button → links directly to Google review page. No incentive. No prompt. No reward. Customer chooses voluntarily. This path NEVER intersects with the raffle.

**Path 2 — Content raffle (Growth tier, incentivised):**
"Snap a photo, enter our raffle" button → opens phone camera → customer takes photo/video of food, drink, venue, experience → uploads via form → photo saves to business's UGC library → customer is entered into weekly prize draw (e.g. free drink or meal).

### Critical: Reviews and Raffle Are Separate

These two paths must NEVER be combined, linked, or cross-referenced:
- The raffle does NOT require or mention leaving a Google review
- Leaving a review does NOT enter the customer into the raffle
- The two buttons are visually and functionally separate on the hub page
- This is how we stay compliant with Google's review policies while still offering customer incentives

### What the Business Gets

- A growing library of authentic customer photos and videos
- Content they can use on Instagram, TikTok, Google Business profile, website, menus, window displays
- Fresh content every week without hiring a photographer or content creator
- A lightweight loyalty mechanism that brings regulars back
- Social proof from real customers in their real venue

### What the Customer Gets

- A chance to win a free drink/meal for snapping a photo they might take anyway
- No purchase necessary to enter (important for UK Gambling Act compliance)
- No email/phone collection required — just a photo and optional first name
- A fun, quick micro-interaction that takes 15 seconds

### Raffle Mechanics

- Business chooses the prize (typically a free drink or meal, cost £3-5)
- Draw happens weekly (business picks winner from entries)
- "No purchase necessary" must be stated on the entry to comply with UK free prize draw rules
- Winner announced on the business's social media (more content, more engagement)
- Voucht does NOT run the raffle — the business does. We provide the entry mechanism.

### Technical Implementation (layered)

**Layer 1 (launch):** "Snap a photo" button on hub page links to a Google Form or Tally form. Customer uploads photo, enters first name. Photos go to a shared Google Drive folder. Business picks winner manually. Zero code needed.

**Layer 2 (month 2-3):** Replace form with custom upload page on Vercel. Branded to business. Photos save to cloud storage (Supabase or Cloudinary). Counter on hub page: "47 photos shared this month."

**Layer 3 (month 4+):** UGC gallery in merchant portal. Business browses, downloads, favourites photos. Video support added. Weekly "content pack" email with best photos ready to post.

## 5. The Hub Page

1. Business logo and name (styled in their brand colour)

- Hub page is hosted by Voucht. Taken down on cancellation.
- Pioneer pages show "Powered by Voucht" footer. Growth pages are white-label.
- Updates via WhatsApp: 48hr turnaround (Pioneer), same-day (Growth).

## 6. The Weekly Competitive Snapshot

### What It Contains

**Pioneer (simpler):**
- Current Google review count and rating
- Change from last week
- Approximate ranking for "[category] near me"
- One actionable recommendation

**Growth (full breakdown):**
- Everything in Pioneer
- Every competitor's name, review count, rating, weekly change
- Ranking position with movement arrows
- Specific competitor to overtake and reviews needed
- Strategic recommendation

### Delivery

Every Monday morning via WhatsApp or email.

### Example (Growth tier)

```
📊 Voucht weekly snapshot — Mosey Cafe

You're currently 4th for "coffee near me" in SE4
⬆️ Up from 5th last week

Reviews: 47 (⭐ 4.6) — gained 3 this week
📸 12 new customer photos in your UGC library

Your area:
1. The Mill — 67 reviews (4.7)
2. Park Life — 55 reviews (4.5)
3. Brockley Beans — 40 reviews (4.4)
4. You — 47 reviews (4.6) ✅
5. Corner Cup — 28 reviews (4.2)

🎯 8 more reviews to overtake Brockley Beans for 3rd

Keep it up — your customers are vouching for you.
```

### Automation Roadmap

**Month 1-3 (manual):** Google Maps search, note rankings, type snapshot, send via WhatsApp. ~10 min/customer.

**Month 3-6 (semi-automated):** Google Sheet stores data, calculates changes, generates text. Copy-paste to WhatsApp. ~3-4 min/customer.

**Month 6+ (fully automated):** Rank tracker or Google Places API scraper. Auto-updates, auto-generates, auto-sends. Zero touch — Jelani reviews before send.

## 11. UK Raffle / Prize Draw Compliance

- The customer photo raffle is a FREE PRIZE DRAW under the Gambling Act 2005
- No purchase necessary to enter (customer just needs to snap a photo — doesn't need to buy anything)
- "No purchase necessary" must be stated on the entry mechanism
- Free prize draws do NOT require a gambling licence in the UK
- The business runs the draw, not Voucht — we provide the entry mechanism
- If the business wants to restrict to paying customers, that becomes a LOTTERY which has different rules — advise them to keep it open
- Winner selection and prize fulfilment is the business's responsibility
